## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time
1. Open Algsoch app
2. Send your first message
3. Messages are saved automatically!

### View Previous Chats
1. Tap the **clock icon** (🕐) in the top right
2. See all your previous conversations
3. Click any to continue

### Export Your Data
1. Tap the **download icon** (⬇️) next to history
2. Choose CSV for spreadsheets or JSON for backup
3. Files appear in your phone's chat_history folder

## ❓ Quick FAQ

**Q: Will I lose my chats?**  
A: No! They're saved automatically.

**Q: Do I need internet?**  
A: No! Everything works offline.

**Q: Where are my files stored?**  
A: In your phone's chat_history folder (inside Files app).

**Q: Can I backup my chats?**  
A: Yes! Use the export feature to save as CSV or JSON.

**Q: What if I don't like a chat?**  
A: Delete it from the History menu with one click.
